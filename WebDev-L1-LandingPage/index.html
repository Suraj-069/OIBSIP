Quick Farming Tips

Simple Tips for Better Results

A few easy practices that can make a noticeable difference in your farm's health and productivity.

01

Test Your Soil First

Understanding your soil's nutrients and pH helps you choose the right crop and the right fertilizer amount.

02

Rotate Your Crops

Growing different crops in sequence keeps soil healthy and naturally reduces pest and disease build-up.

03

Water Early in the Day

Watering in the early morning reduces evaporation loss and helps crops absorb moisture more effectively.

04

Track Market Prices

Keeping an eye on nearby market rates helps you decide the best time and place to sell your harvest.
