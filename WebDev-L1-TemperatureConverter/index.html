CONVERSION GUIDE

Know the Formulas

°C→°F
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
°F→°C
°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9
°C→K
K = °C + 273.15
K→°C
°C = K − 273.15
°F→K
K = (°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
K→°F
°F = (K − 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
